Lemongrass Chicken Recipe

Ingredients

  • Chicken thighs
  • Fish sauce
  • Sugar
  • Thai bird eye chili peppers
  • Mushroom seasoning
  • Lemongrass
  • Minced Garlic

Instructions

  1. Mix all ingredients into a bowl and marinate overnight.
  2. Start up charcoal briquettes for grilling.
  3. On the hottest part of the grill, lay out chicken skin side down first.
  4. Grill for 5 minutes. Then turn 45 degrees and grill another 5 minutes to achieve criss-cross grill marks.
  5. Turn chicken over and repeat previous step.
  6. Check temperature of chicken. If below 155, put lid on top of grill with vents opened and move chicken to indirect heat.
  7. Continue cooking until internal temperateure of chicken reaches 155.
  8. Remove from grill and cover with foil tent. Let coast until internal temperature reaches 160.
  9. Serve and enjoy!
