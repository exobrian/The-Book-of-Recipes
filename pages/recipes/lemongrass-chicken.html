Lemongrass Chicken Recipe (Gà Nướng Sả)

Ingredients

Marinade

  • 3 Pounds of chicken thighs
  • 3 Tablespoons of Fish sauce
  • 1 Tablespoon of Sugar
  • 2 Thai birdeye chili peppers minced
  • 1 Tablespoon of mushroom seasoning or chicken bouillon
  • 3 Tablespoons of Lemongrass minced
  • 1 Tablespoon of Garlic minced

Dipping Sauce

  • 1 Tablespoon of Lime Juice
  • 1 Tablespoon of Water
  • 1 Tablespoon of Fish Sauce
  • 1 Clove of garlic minced
  • 1 Teaspoon of Sambek Garlic Chili
  • 1 Thai birdeye chili minced

Instructions

  1. Mix all Marinade ingredients into a bowl and marinate overnight. Mix all Dipping Sauce ingredients together and store in refrigerator.
  2. Start up charcoal briquettes for grilling and arrange for indirect cooking.
  3. On the hottest part of the grill, lay out chicken skin side down first.
  4. Grill for 5 minutes. Then turn 45 degrees and grill another 5 minutes to achieve criss-cross grill marks.
  5. Flip chicken over and repeat previous step.
  6. Check internal temperature of chicken. If below 155, put lid on top of grill with vents opened and move chicken to indirect heat.
  7. Continue cooking until internal temperateure of chicken reaches 155.
  8. Remove from grill and cover with foil tent. Let coast until internal temperature reaches 160.
  9. Serve with Dipping Sauce and enjoy!
